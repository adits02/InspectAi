How It Works

Discover the step-by-step process behind Nirikshan SahaAI’s powerful inspection capabilities.

Step 1: Data Collection - Our system begins by gathering critical information from various sources, including institutional records, compliance documents, and on-site data inputs. This ensures a comprehensive dataset for analysis.

Step 2: Intelligent Analysis - Leveraging advanced machine learning models, the system analyzes the collected data to identify patterns, inconsistencies, and areas needing improvement. The AI ensures precision and impartiality.

Step 3: Automated Reporting - Once the analysis is complete, Nirikshan SahaAI generates detailed and actionable reports, highlighting key findings, compliance gaps, and tailored recommendations for improvement.

Step 4: Continuous Monitoring - Beyond inspections, our solution facilitates ongoing monitoring, enabling institutions to track progress and make informed decisions for long-term growth.
