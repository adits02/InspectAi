Regional Climate and Classroom Norms

1. Northern Plains - Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan

  • Climate of the Region: Extreme (Hot Summers, Cold Winters)
  • Key Challenges:
    • Temperature Variation
    • High Solar Radiation
    • Dust Protection
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: North-South
    • Recommended Window Area: 15-20% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.0-3.5 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 4-6 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature: 24-26°C

2. Western Coastal - Maharashtra, Goa, Gujarat

  • Climate of the Region: Humid Tropical
  • Key Challenges:
    • High Humidity
    • Monsoon Rains
    • Corrosion Risk
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: East-West with overhangs
    • Recommended Window Area: 20-25% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.5-4.0 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 6-8 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature: 23-25°C

3. Southern Plateau - Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Telangana

  • Climate of the Region: Semi-Arid to Tropical
  • Key Challenges:
    • Temperature Fluctuations
    • Glare Management
    • Dust Control
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: Northeast-Southwest
    • Recommended Window Area: 18-22% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.2-3.7 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 5-7 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature: 24-26°C

4. Eastern Coastal - Odisha, West Bengal, Andhra Pradesh

  • Climate of the Region: Tropical Monsoon
  • Key Challenges:
    • High Rainfall
    • Humidity
    • Cyclone Prone Areas
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: East with deep overhangs
    • Recommended Window Area: 22-27% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.6-4.1 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 7-9 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature: 23-25°C

5. Himalayan Region - Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Jammu & Kashmir

  • Climate of the Region: Alpine to Temperate
  • Key Challenges:
    • Cold Temperature
    • Limited Sunlight
    • Snow Protection
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: South-facing
    • Recommended Window Area: 15-20% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.0-3.5 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 3-5 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature: 22-24°C

6. North-Eastern Hilly - Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Tripura

  • Climate of the Region: Sub-Tropical Monsoon
  • Key Challenges:
    • High Rainfall
    • Humidity
    • Terrain Variations
  • Classroom Norms:
    • Window Orientation: Northeast with side ventilation
    • Recommended Window Area: 20-25% of floor area
    • Ceiling Height: 3.3-3.8 meters
    • Ventilation Rate: 5-7 air changes per hour
    • Thermal Comfort Temperature
